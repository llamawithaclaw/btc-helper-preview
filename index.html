Benchmark history

Simple day-by-day difference vs baseline DCA. Green = outperforming, red = underperforming, gray = in line.

Benchmark boundary: benchmark snippets are intentionally suppressed on degraded days so weak-coverage runs do not create false precision or promotional-looking claims.

How this benchmark works

  • Baseline DCA buys the same fixed daily amount every day.
  • BTC Helper also buys the standard daily amount on Normal and Cautious days.
  • On Pause days, that day’s allocation is held in reserve instead of being bought immediately.
  • On Aggressive days, BTC Helper buys the normal daily amount plus any reserve accumulated during prior Pause days.

This benchmark is meant to test whether BTC Helper improves disciplined accumulation timing relative to ordinary daily DCA, not whether it can perfectly predict price.
